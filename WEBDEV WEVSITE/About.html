About



Aice is a Singaporean ice cream brand that established itself in Indonesia. It’s popular for its low price, which the company credits to the use of local ingredients (apart from imported milk). It popped up rapidly in Metro Manila—and the rest of the country, actually—in the past few years. They even went viral in mid-2019 for their fruit-shaped variants and locally familiar (e.g. sweet corn) flavors; plus the fact their ice cream only ranges from PHP10 to PHP30.
